Temperature conversions are performed by formula, which depends on the two temperature scales you are converting between.

For example, to convert 80 degrees Celsius to Fahrenheit, we put the numbers into the formula as : °F = °C * 9/5 + 32

°F = 80 * 9/5 + 32

°F = 144 + 32

°F = 176

80 degrees Celsius is equal to 176 degrees Fahrenheit.

Formulas for Converting Temperature Scales:

The following temperature conversion formulas are used to convert from one temperature scale to another.

Celsius Temperature Conversion Formula
Celsius to Fahrenheit Conversion °F = °C × 9/5 + 32
Celsius to Kelvin Conversion K = °C + 273.15
Celsius to Rankine Conversion °R = °C × 9/5 + 491.67


Fahrenheit Temperature Conversion Formula
Fahrenheit to Celsius Conversion °C = (°F − 32) × 5/9
Fahrenheit to Kelvin Conversion K = (°F + 491.67) × 5/9
Fahrenheit to Rankine Conversion °R = °F + 491.67


Kelvin Temperature Conversion Formula
Kelvin to Celsius Conversion °C = K − 273.15
Kelvin to Fahrenheit Conversion °F = K × 9/5 − 491.67
Kelvin to Rankine Conversion °R = K × 9/5


Rankine Temperature Conversion Formula
Rankine to Celsius Conversion °C = °R × 5/9 − 273.15
Rankine to Fahrenheit Conversion °F = °R − 491.67
Kelvin Conversion K = °R × 5/9
